Spaghetti Bolognese

Another Italian Classic. If you can't tell by now, I love this type of food.

Everyone needs a great everyday Spaghetti Bolognese recipe, and this is mine! The Bolognese Sauce is rich, thick and has beautiful depth of flavour. Itâ€™s perfect for a quick midweek meal but even better if you can simmer it for a couple of hours!

The Recipe:

  • Rao's Dried Spaghetti Noodles
  • Rao's Tomato Sauce
  • Grass-Fed Ground Beef
  • Diced Garlic Cloves
  • Diced White Onions
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese

How To Cook:

  1. Boil Water in a large pot
  2. Sautee Garlic and Onions
  3. Put Spaghetti in Pot once water is boiling
  4. Place Beef in Pan and cook until light pink
  5. After 10 minutes, drain water and remove spaghetti
  6. Pour Bolognese Sauce over Spaghetti Pot and Mix
  7. It's That Easy!
